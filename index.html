RULES WERE MADE TO BE CHANGED.

Coming real soon.

In Real Winners, you'll operate as a soccer-playing robot. Draw cards that radically change the rules and end up with a game that's nothing like where it started.

Rules cards flip the game upside down with features like adding pinball bumpers around the field, implementing golf rules, or supersizing the ball. By the end of the match the game is nothing like where it started.

Amigos is an independent game studio dedicated to making silly things. Kevin Knight, Jordan Cuffie and Majdi Badri have too many ideas too often and rather than just let those ideas wilt away, they have decided to put them on screen.
